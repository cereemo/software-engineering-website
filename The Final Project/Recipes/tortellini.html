Cheese Tortellini

Ingredients
  • 12 to 16 ounces frozen or refrigerated cheese tortellini
  • 2 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 tea spoon Kosher salt
  • 3 cup baby spinach leaves, tightly packed (or chopped spinach)
  • 8 fresh basil leaves
  • 4 ounce goat cheese log
  • 28 ounce can crushed fire roasted tomatoes (or best quality crushed tomatoes)

Directions

Step 1:

Boil the tortellini according to the package instructions (fresh usually takes 2 to 3 minutes and frozen takes 2 to 5 minutes to boil). Then drain it.


Step 2:

Meanwhile, in a large skillet, place the crushed tomatoes, olive oil, balsamic vinegar, garlic powder, oregano, kosher salt, spinach, and whole basil leaves. Bring to a simmer, then cover and simmer while the pasta cooks or at least 10 minutes.


Step 3:

When the pasta is done and drained, add it to the skillet. Crumble in the goat cheese into the sauce and stir until a creamy sauce forms (if youâ€™d like, save out a few crumbles for topping). Serve immediately.


Step 4:

Serve hot and enjoy!

Nutrition Facts

(per serving)

  • 425 Calories
  • 45.8g Carbs
  • 18.4g Protein
  • 4.4g Sugars
  • 19.4g Fat
