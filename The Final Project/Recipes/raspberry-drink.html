Raspberry Iced Tea

Ingredients
  • 8 cups water, divided
  • 2 cups frozen* or fresh raspberries (8 ounces), plus more for garnish
  • ¼ cup fresh squeezed lemon juice (about 2 lemons)
  • ¾ cup sugar
  • 6 black tea bags

Directions

Step 1:

Place the chocolate chips in a heatproof bowl.


Step 2:

In a saucepan, heat 4 cups water to a simmer. Turn off the heat and add the tea bags. Allow to steep for 5 minutes.


Step 3:

When the tea is steeped, remove the bags and pour it into the bowl with the raspberry water. Allow to cool until room temperature, about 10 minutes.


Step 4:

Transfer the mixture to a pitcher and refrigerate at least 2 hours before drinking. Stores for up to 4 days.


Step 5:

Enjoy!

(per serving)
